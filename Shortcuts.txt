Shortcuts:

!CASE SENSITIVE!

F1 -> F8 - Top bar windows

Production window:
    d - Division Builder
    f - Ship Builder
    a - Air Builder
Technology window:
    r - Toggle research of selected tech

OOB/Units:
    w - jump to selected unit
    h - toggle OOB lines
    n - toggle upgrade priority
    i - toggle reinforcements
    u - toggle upgrades
    l - load on Ship
    p - load on plane
    a - attach to OOB
    d - detach from OOB
    c - create next higher OOB unit
    r - split unit
    g - merge units
